About Me 

I am KAUSHIK Shende, currently pursuing my Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering. My academic journey revolves around exploring emerging technologies, with a special interest in the Internet of Things (IoT), embedded systems, and wireless communication. I enjoy working on how interconnected devices can improve efficiency, safety, and user experience, and I see IoT as a bridge between traditional engineering and modern innovation.

Alongside my studies, I am actively involved in a health tech startup, where I am gaining first-hand experience in sales, marketing, leadership, and accountability. This role has not only sharpened my business acumen but also helped me understand how technology can be transformed into scalable, impactful solutions for real-world problems. It has taught me to balance technical thinking with customer-centric execution.

I aspire to serve in the Indian Air Force through AFCAT or CDS, where I can integrate my engineering skills, IoT knowledge, and leadership experience to contribute to national defence and technological advancement. Whether it’s building smart systems for civil use or applying them in defence scenarios, my vision is to create solutions that make a measurable difference.

Beyond my academic and professional commitments, I am passionate about maintaining a healthy and active lifestyle. I enjoy gym training to stay physically fit, which aligns with my discipline-focused mindset. Traveling allows me to explore new cultures and perspectives, while photography helps me capture unique moments and stories. I also have a love for bike riding, which gives me a sense of freedom and adventure, fueling my curiosity and resilience in life.
